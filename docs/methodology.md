# Methodology

## KPI Definitions
<!-- Fill in as queries are written. Example: -->
- **Active Card Rate** — share of cards with ≥ 1 successful transaction in a given month.
- **Average Ticket Size** — total approved spend / count of approved transactions.
- **MoM Growth** — (current month volume − prior month volume) / prior month volume.

## Assumptions
- Synthetic IBM data; not representative of any real issuer portfolio.
- Declines are inferred from the dataset's transaction status / error field.
- Currency assumed USD throughout.
